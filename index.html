HOW E-SECURE WORKS

This is the Basckend Process of our Panic Button App
, This the E-SECURE is the steps taken from beggining to end of a Panic Alert Response!

  • 1

    PANIC PRESSED

    Client activates a panic from any one of the devices available

  • 2

    CONTROL ALERT

    Control room receives the panic with all client information

  • 3

    RESPONDER LOGGED

    5 closest responder vehicles receive notification to respond

  • 4

    INITIATE RESPONSE

    First responder to acknowledge the panic starts responding to the location

  • 5

    RESPONDER ARRIVES

    Responder arrives on scene with support and information received from the control room

  • 6

    NUETRALISE THREAT

    Responder Completes the response by ensuring the client is safe and secure

  • 7

    COMPLETE THREAT ALERT

    Enteres all details and relevant information into the responder app for record keeping purposes

  • 8

    RETURN TO PATROL

    Panic Logged to Control and Armee Responder Returns to assigned high risk Patrol Areas.
